Philips Interview Experience

Round 1:
Initial Shortlisting - based on Cgpa(7+) followed by Hirevue Round. The Hirevue round consisted of some fun games and some questions based on scenarios, Like a time where you overcame your weakness etc.
Status: Selected

Round 2:
Online Coding Assessment - Had 6 Dsa,5 Dbms,6 Cn, 7 Oops, 6 Os, 5 C-programming based Mcqs followed by 1 Coding Question. Scheduling Question - where a set of tasks to be processed by set of interns, within specified deadline.
Status: Selected

Round 3:
Technical Interview - Started with a breif introduction about myself, followed by some basic questions on my past internship experiences. I was too nervous, but the interviewer was extremely supportive. Asked basic Oops questions, like what are objects and classes, followed by pseudo-code for an example of inheritance, where I had to inherit a parent class called shape and make the child classes such as Rectangle, Triangle extend the parent class followed by method overriding. Next He asked MySql query to inner join some tables. Followed by an Easy Hashmap question which counts the frequency of letters in a string and finally grilled on my projects.
Status: Selected

Round 4:
Managerial Interview - Started again with a breif introduction about myself, followed by some questions on my past internship experiences and dived deep into my projects. Followed by some basic Oops questions. Then she asked me about challenges faced in my past internships and the steps I took to overcome them. Then she gave me some imaginary situations and what actions would I perform in that pressurized situation. Overall this round lasted around 45 mins.

Status: Awaiting Results

Motorola Interview Experience

Round 1:
Online Assessment - This round consisted of 20 MCQs based on Python- Syntax, Differences, etc. Followed by 5 easy Coding Questions. I managed to solve all 5 questions. All were to be completed in 1 hour
Status: Selected

Round 2:
Technical Interview - Panel of 2 interviewers - Started with a breif introduction about myself, Dived straight to DSA question, which was to find the leader in an array.

Initially I built brute force, O(N^2) which I was asked to optimize, However, I got struck and asked for an hint, which they gave me to start from the right end, after the hint, I was immediately able to solve it. After this, they asked me questions related to OOPs, CN and OS. After this, he started asking me questions related to Projects, which even I was hearing for the first time, however, I did answer, as the tech stack was somewhat similar to my project. But when he specified the tech stack for next project, I was sure this wasn't my project, I polietly requested him to recheck the resume, after which we came to know that he accidently mismatched the resume, I took a sigh of relief.

And then I regained my lost confidence, and explained my rest of the projects, and how there was a tech stack similarity between the other guy's resume! After this they asked if I had any queries for them, (P.S Do your homework here!) - I asked him that since they are using Hadoop as their tech stack, why was NO question even near to Hadoop/DS in the interview and he explained. (My guess is this part impressed him the most!)

Status: Accepted Intern Offer
